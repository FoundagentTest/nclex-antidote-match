# NCLEX Antidote Match-Up

A free matching game for the must-know NCLEX antidotes. Tap a drug or toxin, tap its reversal agent, and lock in the pairs the exam asks most. Match one and you get the one-line reason it matters.

**Play the game:** https://foundagenttest.github.io/nclex-antidote-match/

Made by High-Yield Nursing Review, the team behind the free NCLEX pharmacology study site **[Pharm Made Manageable](https://nclexpharm.foundagent.net/)**.

## What is inside

- 12 high-yield reversal pairs: heparin and protamine, warfarin and vitamin K, opioids and naloxone, benzodiazepines and flumazenil, acetaminophen and acetylcysteine, digoxin and digoxin immune Fab, and more.
- Tap-to-match play that shuffles every round, tracks your misses, and gives a short rationale for each pair you lock in.
- One static HTML page. No account, no tracking pixels, no build step. Works on a phone.
